## LocoTrainer-4b: Code analysis with integrated markdown reporting.

LocoTrainer-4B is a specialized 4-billion parameter AI model that generates detailed markdown reports directly from codebase analysis, achieved through knowledge distillation from Qwen3-Coder-Next. This eliminates the need for an additional reasoning model, differentiating it from general code analysis tools.

## Canadian AI funding boosts manufacturing competitiveness.

Next Generation Manufacturing Canada (NGen) is investing $79.5 million in new AI projects to boost Canadian manufacturers' global competitiveness. This includes $29.2 million in federal funding to support 20 initiatives focused on adopting and scaling Canadian AI solutions within the manufacturing sector.
