## AI assists, not replaces, cybersecurity experts.

The US government is adopting AI to boost cybersecurity efforts, but experts warn that Generative AI should be used to augment, not replace, human cybersecurity professionals. This approach aims to leverage AI's capabilities while ensuring critical human oversight remains in place.
