## Deepfake AI impersonation arrests in Ghana.

Ghanaian police have arrested 11 individuals, including Nigerians, for allegedly using AI-generated deepfake videos to impersonate former President John Dramani Mahama. The arrests were part of coordinated cyber operations in the Volta Region.
