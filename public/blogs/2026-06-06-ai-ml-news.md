# AI/ML News Updates

Stay informed with the latest developments in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

## Chinese labs lead video AI race against OpenAI.

Chinese AI labs are emerging as leaders in video generation, with companies like ByteDance and Alibaba developing powerful models that rival and even surpass those previously pioneered by OpenAI. This surge in Chinese innovation highlights a competitive landscape where domestic advancements are rapidly shaping the future of video AI.

---

## China telecoms pilots open to foreign investment.

China is easing foreign ownership restrictions in its telecom sector, removing a 50% cap and allowing foreign companies to operate independently in pilot regions. This move aims to attract investment and counter US restrictions by fostering a more open market for telecommunications services.

---

## WWDC 2026: Siri, AI, and major iOS 27 upgrades.

Apple's WWDC 2026 is expected to feature a significant overhaul of Siri, transforming it into a full-fledged chatbot with enhanced AI capabilities and a new system-wide search interface. The event will also showcase AI-powered updates across iOS and macOS, including advanced photo editing, writing tools, and improved Siri integrations.

---

## ISS air leak, crew shelters in Dragon capsule.

NASA crew temporarily evacuated the ISS due to a significant air leak, taking shelter in a Dragon capsule. While one of two newly discovered leaks has been fixed, another remains unrepaired, though operations have returned to normal.

---

## AI recommendations drive Meesho's product discovery

Meesho now sees over 75% of its orders driven by AI-powered recommendations from its PRISM engine. This engine is revolutionizing product discovery by personalizing user feeds, leading to the majority of platform sales.
