## AI/ML for Nano/Pico Scale Device Development

A lecture at CU Meet highlighted the crucial role of AI and ML in developing nano and pico-scale devices, as explained by Prof. Srinivasa Rao Pentyala from IIT Dhanbad. This discussion focused on how these advanced technologies are driving innovation in miniaturized device creation.

## AI: Life sciences infrastructure for drug development.

AI is rapidly becoming essential infrastructure in the life sciences, moving beyond pilot projects to production-grade tools. These systems are now actively shaping drug design, manufacturing, submission, and approval processes.

## AI streamlines motor control with STMicroelectronics software.

STMicroelectronics has launched a new machine learning software pack that simplifies the integration of AI into motor control systems. This allows for easier optimization and predictive maintenance in applications like industrial drives, home appliances, and robotics.
