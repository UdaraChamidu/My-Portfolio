## AI making high-stakes decisions with little transparency.

AI is increasingly making critical decisions in areas like hiring, education, and entertainment, often with opaque processes and questionable effectiveness. This trend raises concerns about transparency and accountability as algorithms mediate human access to jobs, knowledge, and entertainment.

## Federated learning trains AI without sharing patient data

A new federated learning approach allows hospitals to train AI models for better medical diagnosis without ever sharing sensitive patient data. This breakthrough enables global collaboration by sending the AI to the data, rather than the data to the AI.
