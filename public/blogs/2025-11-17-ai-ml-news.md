## India's AI: Performance, Multiple Use Cases, GenAI Ready

An EY-CII report indicates India's AI adoption is maturing beyond pilot programs, with 47% of enterprises now using multiple AI cases. Furthermore, 76% of business leaders anticipate a significant impact from GenAI, with 63% feeling ready to leverage it effectively.

---

## India AI: Shift to Performance, Modest Investment

Indian enterprises are shifting their AI focus from pilot projects to performance, with 47% already leveraging multiple AI use cases. However, despite this optimism, investments remain modest, as over 95% of organizations dedicate less than 20% of their IT budgets to AI.

---

## Indian GenAI Adoption High, Budgets Low

Nearly half of Indian enterprises are actively deploying generative AI in production. However, AI/ML budgets remain sparse, with over 95% allocating less than 20% of their overall IT spending.

## Cloud FinOps Market Booms to $27B by 2032

The Cloud FinOps market is projected to reach USD 27.67 billion by 2032, growing from USD 12.18 billion in 2024 at a 10.8% CAGR, according to Credence Research. This growth highlights the increasing demand for solutions that integrate technology, processes, and culture to enhance visibility and control over cloud spending.
