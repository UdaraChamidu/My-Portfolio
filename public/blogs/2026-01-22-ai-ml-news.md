## Functional Fluids Market Growth to $64 Billion

The global functional fluids market is projected to grow significantly, reaching $64.27 billion by 2031 from $43.46 billion in 2023, driven by major players like ExxonMobil and Shell. This expansion is expected to occur at a compound annual growth rate of 5.08%.
