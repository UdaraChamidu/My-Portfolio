## Hedge funds shift focus to mid-cap AI stocks.

Hedge funds are shifting their AI investment focus from mega-cap tech stocks, with their high valuations, to mid-cap AI companies. They believe mid-caps offer greater potential for significant returns as they have more room to grow and catch up to larger players in the AI sector.

---

## Musk reorganizes xAI for AI dominance.

Elon Musk is reportedly reorganizing xAI's talent after several engineers departed, framing it as a strategic move to accelerate the company's pursuit of AI dominance. This talent reshuffle comes as xAI aims for faster execution and increased leadership effectiveness.

---

## xAI Exits: Push or Pull? Musk Weighs In

Elon Musk claims recent departures from xAI were a "push" rather than a "pull," implying engineers left by their own volition due to internal issues rather than being lured away. This comes amid significant controversy and a wave of at least nine engineer exits from the company in the past week.

## AI and Analytics for Supply Chain Optimization

ISG is launching a study to assess providers offering AI and analytics solutions that improve supply chain operations. These providers leverage data-driven AI to optimize workflows and enhance supply chain execution.

## ISG AI/Analytics Supply Chain Provider Report

ISG is launching a research study to evaluate AI and analytics providers that help businesses create more predictive and efficient supply chains. The findings, published in a May 2026 report, will assist enterprises in assessing vendors and improving their supply chain operations through data-driven solutions.
