## Log math beats Nvidia's multiplications for AI.

Tensordyne is developing AI hardware that utilizes log math instead of multiplications, betting on this optimization to outperform Nvidia's compute-intensive solutions. This approach aims to achieve better performance by leveraging the additive property of logarithms.
