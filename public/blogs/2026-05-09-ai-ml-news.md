## Programmers' evolving trust and job fears with AI

Developers are increasingly integrating AI into their daily workflows, showing a growing trust in its capabilities. However, this adoption is mixed, with many still using AI sporadically or not at all, and a lingering concern that AI might eventually replace their jobs.

## AI training for 500 officers in coal sector.

IIM Sambalpur is partnering with Mahanadi Coalfields Limited to train 500 officers in AI and ML. This initiative aims to drive digital transformation and innovation within the Indian coal sector.

## AI automates network management tasks, evolving roles

AI is increasingly embedded in network management, automating tasks like anomaly detection, fault detection, and incident ticket creation. This shift frees up network professionals to focus on higher-level responsibilities like system design and governance, rather than manual data analysis.
