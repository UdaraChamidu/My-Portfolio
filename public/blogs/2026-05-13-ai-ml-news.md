## Army considers plant-based battlefield nutrition

The US Army is exploring plant-based protein options, like powders and gels, to enhance soldier nutrition and potentially improve readiness. This move signals a shift towards more sustainable and versatile food sources for military personnel.

## FCC extends router update waivers to 2029

The FCC has extended waivers for router updates until 2029, reversing a planned ban that could have left millions of devices vulnerable and unpatched. This decision was made after the FCC realized the ban would have significantly impacted network security.

## FCC extends router update waivers to 2029

The FCC has reversed its decision to ban router firmware updates, extending waivers until 2029. This move prevents millions of devices from becoming vulnerable to security risks.

## Congress probes Canvas breach, CEO faces questions.

Instructure, the company behind the Canvas learning platform, is facing a congressional investigation following a data breach where they paid a ransom. CEO Steve Daly will be questioned about the security incident.
