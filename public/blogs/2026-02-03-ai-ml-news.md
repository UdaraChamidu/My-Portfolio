## Snowflake partners OpenAI for AI-powered databases.

Snowflake is investing $200 million in OpenAI to integrate their AI models directly into Snowflake's data platform. This partnership aims to make databases more conversational and accessible through features like Cortex AI and Snowflake Intelligence, bypassing their previous reliance on Azure.
