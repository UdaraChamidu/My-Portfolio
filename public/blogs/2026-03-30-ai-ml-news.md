The future of software is shifting towards Agents-as-a-Service (AaaS) by 2026, moving beyond traditional SaaS to autonomous AI agents that handle tasks through an "agentic stack" architecture. Tech companies are laying off tens of thousands of employees in early 2026, reallocating billions from labor costs into AI infrastructure. This shift in spending benefits capital expenditure owners rather than the displaced workforce.
