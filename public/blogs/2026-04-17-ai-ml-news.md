## AHOY's Real-World AI Surpasses $100M Revenue

## AI deepfake detection, cost-effective call monitoring

Modulate's velma-2 model has achieved the top spot on Hugging Face's speech deepfake leaderboard, detecting 98.9% of fakes with a highly efficient and cost-effective architecture. This breakthrough comes as voice deepfake losses are projected to skyrocket, making advanced detection tools like velma-2 critical for combating fraud.

## AI readiness gap: India's engineers not truly AI-ready.

Scaler has launched India's first fully AI-native tech career platform, a significant overhaul driven by their finding that only 19% of engineers are truly AI-ready despite 89% believing they are. This platform aims to bridge that confidence-capability gap by integrating AI into its core curriculum and programs.
