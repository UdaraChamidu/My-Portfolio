## CNN hires NYT data chief for AI leadership.

CNN has hired former New York Times chief data scientist Chris Wiggins to lead their newly created Machine Learning and AI Science department. This move signifies CNN's growing investment in leveraging AI and data science for their news operations.

## AI for sustainable chemical plant audits.

AI and Industry 4.0 technologies like IoT and real-time analytics are being integrated into chemical plant process audits to enhance accuracy and operational efficiency. This focus on sustainability and optimization aims to improve how these audits are conducted.
