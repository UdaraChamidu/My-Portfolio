## LisaFPGA: Open-source Apple misfire reborn.

LisaFPGA is an open-source project recreating Apple's failed Lisa computer on programmable logic, offering a much cheaper alternative and potentially supporting original storage devices. This initiative aims to revive the Lisa's unique architecture in a more accessible and functional way.
