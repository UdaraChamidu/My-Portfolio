## Usage-based insurance market to reach $267.4 billion.

The Usage-Based Insurance market is projected to grow significantly, reaching $267.4 billion by 2032. This growth is driven by various policy types, technologies like OBD-II and smartphones, and applications across new and used vehicles.

## AI robots tackle farm labour, boost yields.

ICAR-IARI has developed AI-powered robots to address farm labor shortages and increase crop yields. These robots analyze field conditions to make real-time decisions, optimizing agriculture.

---

## Threads' Dear Algo lets users shape feeds.

Meta's new "Dear Algo" feature on Threads uses AI to let users influence their "For You" feed for three days. Users can simply post "Dear Algo" followed by their preferences to guide the algorithm's recommendations.

## Financial AI agents to get citable corporate data.

ML model provider Model ML has acquired Captide to integrate their citable corporate disclosure data, enhancing financial AI agents with reliable information. This acquisition aims to improve the accuracy and trustworthiness of AI-driven financial analysis.

---

## AI agents get citable financial disclosure data.

Model ML has acquired Captide, a financial data layer, to enhance its AI agents with citable corporate disclosure data. This acquisition will allow Model ML's AI agents to more reliably access, analyze, and cite global financial filings.
