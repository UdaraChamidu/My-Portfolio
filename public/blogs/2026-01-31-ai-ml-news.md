## Microsoft rethinks Windows 11 AI integration

Microsoft is reportedly reevaluating its "AI Everywhere" strategy in Windows 11 due to user backlash. The company is reviewing how and where AI features, particularly Copilot integrations, are implemented in the operating system.

---

## Hugging Face platform hijacked for Android malware.

The AI platform Hugging Face has been compromised, with attackers using it to distribute Android malware that can seize control of infected devices. This security incident highlights a new vector for malicious actors to spread malware through popular AI development tools.

## AI/ML advances addiction recovery ecosystem

Orbiit Recovery Ecosystem is leveraging AI and machine learning to transform addiction recovery by using behavior-derived intelligence to improve support, measurement, and sustained recovery. This advancement aims to revolutionize how individuals overcome addiction.

## Scaling AI: From Pilot to Production, A Business System

AI initiatives often fail not due to flawed models, but due to challenges in integrating them into existing business systems and operational processes. To successfully scale AI and achieve ROI, focus on designing the overall business system, rather than just the AI model itself.
