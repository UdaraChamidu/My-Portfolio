## GeM's GMV crosses Rs 18.4 lakh crore milestone

The Government e Marketplace (GeM) has surpassed Rs 18.4 lakh crore in cumulative Gross Merchandise Value. This milestone, with over Rs 5 lakh crore in FY26 alone, highlights strong confidence in GeM's transparent, tech-driven procurement system.

---

## GeM hits Rs 18.4 lakh crore GMV, aided by SMEs.

The Government e Marketplace (GeM) has reached a significant milestone, surpassing Rs 18.4 lakh crore in cumulative Gross Merchandise Value. This achievement highlights the platform's growing importance in facilitating trade, with a substantial portion of its Rs 5 lakh crore GMV in FY25-26 driven by Micro and Small Enterprises (MSEs) and women-led businesses.

## UiPath unveils agentic automation and Maestro.

UiPath is moving beyond traditional RPA towards "agentic automation" with its new roadmap. This strategy emphasizes Maestro orchestration and the development of coding agents to enhance their automation platform.

## Healthcare recruitment drive to strengthen system.

Prime Minister Tarique Rahman announced a phased recruitment of 100,000 healthcare workers nationwide to build a humane healthcare system. This initiative aims to bolster the country's healthcare infrastructure.
