## Zoom CEO: AI frees us for 3-day work week.

Zoom's CEO is advocating for a three-day work week, believing AI advancements will eventually allow employees to earn more leisure time. He suggests that the traditional five-day schedule is no longer necessary due to AI's potential to boost productivity.

## Adobe India AI Research Fellowship applications open

Adobe India has opened applications for its AI Research Fellowship for outstanding PhD students, offering a Rs 12 lakh annual stipend and research opportunities in AI and related fields. Interested candidates can apply online before April 30th.

## Pharma AI lab Helical raises €8.4 million.

Pharma R&D teams are struggling to translate AI predictions into actionable science, and Helical aims to bridge this gap. The company has secured €8.4 million in seed funding to create a collaborative virtual AI lab that connects computational and biological insights.

## OpenAI seeks new partners beyond Microsoft.

OpenAI is seeking to diversify its partnerships, stating that Microsoft's influence has "limited our ability" to build a customer base, while expressing optimism about a new relationship with Amazon. Despite Microsoft's crucial role in OpenAI's growth, the AI company is actively pursuing new collaborations.
