## GPU Upscaling Tech: DLSS, FSR, XeSS Comparison

NVIDIA's DLSS, AMD's FSR, and Intel's XeSS are GPU vendor technologies designed to boost gaming performance and visual quality. They achieve this through techniques like temporal upscaling and frame generation, using machine learning to render more realistic and smoother graphics, especially with demanding effects like ray tracing.

## DLSS 5: Impressive Tech, AI Slop Concerns

A modder for "RTGI" acknowledges the impressive technology behind NVIDIA DLSS 5 but expresses significant concern that it's an "AI slop" that compromises artistic vision. This highlights a strong debate among gamers regarding the impact of advanced AI upscaling on game graphics and artistic intent.

## Google secures energy for AI data centres.

Google is securing power for its AI data centers by signing long-term energy contracts with three US utilities, adding 1 gigawatt of demand response capacity. This move aims to ensure a stable energy supply for their growing data center needs.
