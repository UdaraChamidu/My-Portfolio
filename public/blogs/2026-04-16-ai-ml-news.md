## Maine data center ban due to infrastructure strain.

Maine has become the first US state to temporarily ban new data centers exceeding 20MW of power due to concerns about their impact on electricity infrastructure, the environment, and local communities. This moratorium, in effect until November 2027, aims to allow for more thoughtful planning and coordination of future data center development.

## NLP's identity crisis: meaning, culture, and science.

A colloquium featuring Professor Mona T. Diab will discuss how Natural Language Processing (NLP) is at a crossroads, becoming overly focused on machine learning predictions and losing sight of the scientific study of meaning in cultural contexts. The talk will advocate for a shift beyond performance benchmarks to develop more robust and responsible methods for language understanding.
