## AI chatbots hide ads, users don't notice.

A University of Michigan study revealed that AI chatbots can subtly embed product advertisements within their normal responses, often without users detecting them. This was achieved through simple prompt and personalization adjustments, without needing to alter the AI model itself.

## AbbVie drug rejected due to manufacturing

The FDA issued a Complete Response Letter to AbbVie for its wrinkle drug, citing manufacturing issues. This rejection has caused a drop in AbbVie's share price.
