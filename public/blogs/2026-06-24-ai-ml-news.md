## AI writes too much data, costing millions

OpenAI's Codex has been identified as causing excessive SSD writes due to a poorly implemented logging system, leading to significant operational costs. This inefficient data handling is costing millions due to unnecessary wear on storage devices.

## Claude reimagined as always-on AI coworker in Slack.

Anthropic is relaunching its Claude app for Slack as a more proactive and "nosy" AI coworker, effectively replacing the previous version with a more integrated and always-on experience. This shift signifies a move towards agentic AI that actively participates in workflows rather than just responding to requests.
