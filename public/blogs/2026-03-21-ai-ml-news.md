## PlayStation adopts AI frame generation for future consoles.

Sony's PlayStation lead architect, Mark Cerny, has confirmed that AI-powered frame generation technology will be implemented in future PlayStation consoles, potentially appearing on the PS6. This controversial tech aims to enhance gaming performance by using machine learning to generate new frames.

## Snowflake pivots to AI, cuts staff.

Snowflake is cutting 700 employees to fund a major AI push, reallocating resources from sales to hire AI engineers amidst fierce competition. This strategic shift aims to position Snowflake for the future of data and AI.

## Quest Global AI Engineer Off Campus Recruitment

Quest Global is actively recruiting AI Engineers through off-campus placements, offering opportunities for fresh graduates. This recruitment drive was initially advertised on Job4freshers.

## AI revolutionizing India's military capabilities.

India's military is rapidly integrating AI across its operations, from training and curriculum development to strategic decision-making and wargaming. This adoption signifies a significant enhancement of India's defense capabilities through data-driven approaches.

## DLSS 5: AI inferring on 2D screencaps, not geometry.

Nvidia's DLSS 5 generates visual enhancements by analyzing 2D screenshots and motion vectors with a generative AI, bypassing traditional game assets like geometry and textures. This approach allows the AI to determine the final rendered appearance with minimal artist control over the output.
