## AI maps Alzheimer's chemical disruption beyond plaques.

AI combined with laser imaging has created the first complete molecular atlas of an Alzheimer's brain, revealing widespread chemical changes beyond amyloid plaques. These changes significantly affect memory regions, suggesting Alzheimer's is a metabolic disruption affecting the entire brain.
