# AI/ML News Updates

Stay informed with the latest developments in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

---

## Nvidia-Google Cloud AI factory superstack.

Nvidia and Google Cloud have partnered to create an "AI factory," integrating Google's AI infrastructure with Nvidia's latest hardware and software. This collaboration aims to expand the distribution of Nvidia's AI computing capabilities.

---

## AWS leadership strengthened amid AI surge.

Amid a surge in AI, Amazon has strengthened AWS leadership by adding infrastructure VP Prasad Kalyanaraman to its S-team and promoting Dave Brown to SVP of compute and ML services. These moves underscore the company's focus on AI-driven growth and customer needs.

---

## Banks' AI race against fintech innovation

Traditional banks face increasing pressure from agile fintechs and neobanks due to their slow modernization. To remain competitive, banks must adopt specific AI strategies to match the rapid innovation seen in the financial technology sector.

---

## Kubernetes 1.36 Haru: AI, Stability, and Enhancements

Kubernetes 1.36 "Haru" significantly boosts AI workload support with DRA maturity and enhances stability through user namespaces and VolumeGroupSnapshots. The release also mandates audits for deprecated features like gitRepo and Ingress NGINX.

---

## Custom AI development replaces off-the-shelf solutions.

AI product development is shifting away from generic, off-the-shelf solutions that primarily wrap existing APIs. Instead, businesses are increasingly opting for custom AI development to create tailored solutions that go beyond simple prompt engineering.
