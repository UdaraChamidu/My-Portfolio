## Hardware for efficient sparse AI computation.

Researchers have developed a new hardware design, Onyx, that significantly improves AI efficiency by leveraging "sparsity," where many parameters in AI models are zero. This innovative hardware, built from the ground up for sparsity, drastically reduces energy consumption and speeds up computations compared to existing hardware like CPUs and GPUs.

## Federated ML: Healthcare's privacy-preserving AI advantage.

Federated Machine Learning offers healthcare organizations a competitive edge by enabling AI model training without centralizing sensitive patient data, thus mitigating privacy risks. This approach allows models to learn from distributed data sources while keeping the data localized.
