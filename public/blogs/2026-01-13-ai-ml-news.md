## AI/ML powering Gujarat's renewable energy future.

Gujarat's renewable energy sector is set to be revolutionized by AI and Machine Learning, as discussed at the Vibrant Gujarat Regional Conference. These advanced technologies will be crucial for smarter energy integration and storage solutions, paving the way for a more efficient and future-ready energy landscape.

## Pattern CEO to discuss AI in Life Science

Pattern Computer's CEO, Mark R. Anderson, will speak at the Pitchforce JPM Investment Summit on "AI in Life Science." This participation highlights the company's role in leveraging AI within the biotechnology sector.

## AI builds custom MRI viewer from DICOM files.

Shopify CEO Tobi Lutke used Anthropic's Claude AI with a single prompt to create a personal MRI viewer. This AI-powered tool processed DICOM files, organized radiology reports and images, and generated a browser-based application.

## Gujarat's renewable energy powered by AI and ML.

Gujarat is leveraging AI and machine learning to enhance its renewable energy sector at the VGRC (Gujarat Renewable Energy Development Agency). This initiative aims to optimize the performance and integration of renewable energy sources for a more sustainable future.

## Gujarat: Clean Energy & Cable Tech Advances

At the Vibrant Gujarat Regional Conference in Rajkot, a panel discussion highlighted advancements in clean energy and cable technology. Industry leaders and academics explored how innovative cable solutions can support the growth of renewable energy and last-mile connectivity.
