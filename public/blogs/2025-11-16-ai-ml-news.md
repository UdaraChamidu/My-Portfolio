## AI Vidya: AI Training for Underserved Youth

Raj Varma has launched AI Vidya, a national initiative aiming to train 1 lakh (100,000) youth from underserved communities in AI. It seeks to make AI education accessible through scholarships, mentorship, and industry-ready training programs.
