## AI software for radiological triage receives FDA clearance.

MedCognetics has secured FDA 510(k) clearance for its CogNet AI-MT+ software, an AI-powered tool designed for radiological computer-aided triage and notification. This clearance allows the company to offer an advanced AI solution to help identify and alert clinicians about critical findings in medical imaging.
