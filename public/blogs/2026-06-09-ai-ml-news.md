## PQC investment competes with AI spending.

Credit rating giant S&P Global has issued a stark warning that spending on post-quantum cryptography (PQC) will directly compete with AI investments, while Google and Cloudflare are accelerating their PQC adoption. This highlights a growing tension between the need for future-proofing digital security and the allure of immediate AI advancements.
