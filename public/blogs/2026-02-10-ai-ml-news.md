## AI's dual role in cybersecurity: threat and defense.

AI presents a dual-edged sword in cybersecurity, empowering both defenders with advanced tools and attackers with more sophisticated, autonomous threats. This evolving landscape makes AI a crucial but challenging battleground for protecting digital assets.

## ELIZA's NLP legacy: a chatbot pioneer

ELIZA, an early AI chatbot, pioneered natural language processing by mimicking therapist conversations with predefined responses. While basic compared to today's AI which uses advanced NLP, ML, and NLG, it laid foundational groundwork.
