## AI platforms for power and energy market intelligence

AI platforms for power and energy market intelligence are categorized into four groups: weather-first forecasters for short-term predictions, long-horizon curve houses for multi-decade revenue modeling, benchmark assessors for contract prices, and environmental attribute intelligence platforms for forecasting. These platforms provide specialized insights across different time scales and market segments within the power and energy industry.

## AI Calling: Transforming telecom into an intelligent service hub

ZTE's AI new calling initiative aims to create an open and intelligent telecom ecosystem, shifting basic voice calls into AI-powered service hubs. This "Calling as a Service" model eliminates the need for app downloads and opens up new revenue streams for operators.

The global data analytics outsourcing market is projected for massive growth, expected to surge from $19.78 billion in 2025 to $551.05 billion by 2036, driven by major players like Wipro and IBM. This rapid expansion represents a compound annual growth rate of 35.3% over the next decade.
