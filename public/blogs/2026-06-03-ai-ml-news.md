## Ransomware operators warned against targeting Russia, CIS

## Government selects AI trusted partners for funding

The Trump administration's proposed AI E-(I)-O would grant the government significant power to designate "trusted partners" for AI development, raising concerns among policy experts about potential favoritism and government interference in the market. This move could allow federal agencies to pick winners and losers in the AI industry by controlling access to critical resources and partnerships.
